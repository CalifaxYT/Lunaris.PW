IN-GAME RULES

• You must be 15+ to join.

• LunarisMC is supposed to be a community. Any form of sexism, racism, or other discrimination/bigotry/attacks/harassment will not be tolerated.

• No modifying other people's stuff without their permission. This is basically "no griefing" and "no stealing" rolled into one. This shouldn't be a rule that I need to explain.

• Don't be a dick.

• No cheating/hacking. (Minor client mods like Minimap, Optifine, InventoryTweaks, Tweakeroo etc are fine.)

• While players are allowed to use a spectator account if they wish (for reasonable purposes such as recording build timelapses), abuse of spectator mode, such as keeping chunks loaded to run farms, is strictly prohibited, and in extreme scenarios, may even result in a ban. Play fair.

• While duplication of gravity blocks can be (half-assedly) justified, duplication of shulker boxes can be used to create infinite amounts of literally any item in the game, and is thus inherently gamebreaking and not allowed.

• Duplicated items that are allowed (such as gravel/sand etc) are only allowed for personal use, and may not be sold or otherwise injected into the economy.

• No "not-safe-for-work" (e.g. pornographic, gorey, etc) content in Discord. Please also shy away from heavily political or religious discussion. This is a game, let's try to have fun instead of bring up things that will just cause arguments.

• All punishment/action is always up to 98w's discretion. There will be no evading justice on a technicality.

• Less of a rule and more of a tip; if you want to stay in LunarisMC without issue, the number one goal is just to do your best to be likeable. If other server members appreciate you and are glad you're a part of the experience, there's almost zero chance you'll ever be asked to leave. Just stay on people's good sides and avoid drama. :)
